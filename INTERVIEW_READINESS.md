# Interview Readiness Guide – AI Infrastructure Senior Architect

## Purpose
Support interview preparation centred on multi-organisation strategy, executive storytelling, and portfolio leadership as captured in the senior architect repository.

## Core Competencies to Highlight
- Aligning multiple business units on shared AI infrastructure strategy  
- Portfolio governance, risk management, and regulatory oversight  
- Executive and board-level communication with measurable outcomes  
- Mentoring architects and coordinating global programmes

## Preparation Checklist
1. Select 2–3 strategic initiatives that demonstrate cross-org impact.  
2. Gather artefacts: strategy canvases, stakeholder maps, escalation frameworks, financial models.  
3. Rehearse executive-level narratives—focus on “why,” trade-offs, and measurable results.  
4. Prepare examples of mentorship, succession planning, and culture-building.

## Mock Interview Structure (60 minutes)
| Segment | Objective | Repo Guidance |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 10 min | Strategic introduction | Root README |
| 20 min | Multi-org strategy deep dive | Strategic modules |
| 15 min | Governance / compliance scenario | Risk and governance notes |
| 10 min | Leadership & mentorship discussion | Leadership frameworks |
| 5 min | Candidate questions | Below |

## Sample Technical Prompts
- “Explain how you unify multiple organisations under a single AI infrastructure vision.”  
- “How do you balance innovation with regulatory and budget constraints?”  
- “Walk through the metrics you use to evaluate portfolio health.”

## Behavioral Prompts
- Handling executive disagreement across regions  
- Steering large-scale transformation or consolidation efforts  
- Coaching principal architects and ensuring succession plans

## Questions to Ask the Interviewer
- “How is strategic alignment measured across your AI infrastructure programmes?”  
- “What governance bodies or councils would I contribute to?”  
- “How do you handle regional or subsidiary differences in requirements?”

## Next Steps
Maintain a curated package of strategy decks, governance artefacts, and success metrics. Update this guide after each major initiative or executive presentation.***
