# Role Requirements: Senior AI Infrastructure Architect

**Level:** 4
**Experience Level:** 12+ years in technology leadership roles, with 5+ years as an enterprise architect or principal architect in AI/ML domains
**Market Demand:** Extreme Shortage - Very limited pool of qualified senior architects with enterprise AI platform experience. High competition among Fortune 500 companies, major tech firms, and consulting organizations for top talent.
**Salary Range (US, 2025):** $200,000 - $500,000 (median $275,000)

- Establish AI infrastructure governance and operating models
- Drive strategic technology partnerships and vendor relationships
- Lead innovation and R&D for next-generation AI infrastructure
- Architect solutions for complex cross-organizational AI initiatives
- Provide executive advisory on AI infrastructure investments and roadmap
- Define and evolve enterprise architecture standards for AI/ML
